Our Projects

Our projects reflect innovation, teamwork, and hands-on engineering. From autonomous robots to IoT-based smart systems, we build solutions that solve real-world problems and compete at national-level events.

Line Following Car

Year: 2023

An autonomous robot that follows a predefined path using IR sensors and PID control. Designed for speed optimization and stability in competitions.

Tech: Arduino, IR Sensors, DC Motors, PID, C++

Obstacle Avoiding Robot

Year: 2023

A smart robot capable of detecting and avoiding obstacles in real time using ultrasonic sensors and decision logic.

Tech: Arduino, Ultrasonic Sensor, Servo Motor, C++

Smart Home IoT System

Year: 2023

Home automation system with voice control, energy monitoring, and predictive maintenance features.

Tech: Raspberry Pi, MQTT, Node.js, Android

Smart Irrigation System

Year: 2022

An automated irrigation system that optimizes water usage based on soil moisture and weather data.

Tech:Soil Sensor, ESP8266, IoT Cloud
