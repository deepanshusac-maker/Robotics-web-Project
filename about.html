About Our Club

Welcome to Robotics Club

Founded in 2015, the Robotics Club has been at the forefront of innovation and technology education. We are a passionate community of students, educators, and robotics enthusiasts dedicated to exploring the fascinating world of robotics, artificial intelligence, and automation.

Mission

Fostering innovation and technical excellence in robotics through hands-on learning and collaborative projects.

vision

To be a leading hub for robotics innovation, producing skilled engineers who shape the future of technology.

Objective

Promote STEM education, encourage research, and provide platform for practical skill development.

Club Milestone


  • 2015 - Club Founded

    • Started with 15 passionate students and one faculty advisor

  • 2017 - First National Win

    • Won first place at National Robotics Competition

  • 2019 - Lab Expansion

    • Upgraded facilities with AI development lab and 3D printing station

  • 2021 - International Recognition

    • Team qualified for World Robotics Championship

  • 2023- Research Publication

    • First IEEE conference paper published by club members
